# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Women and families in Da Nang seeking clear, trustworthy support through pregnancy, birth, postpartum, maternal wellness, strength training, and nutrition. Visitors may be comparing services while navigating unfamiliar or emotionally sensitive stages and need to understand the available support without pressure.

## Product Purpose

Present DaNang Birth Collective as a connected, personal practice and help visitors understand the five available areas of support, learn about the practitioner-led approach, access the Cross-Cultural Guide to Birth, and begin a conversation. Success means visitors quickly understand what is offered, feel safe and informed, and can confidently request information.

## Brand Personality

Calm, capable, and human. The experience should feel steady and professional without becoming clinical, decorative, overly feminine, or impersonal.

## Anti-references

- Generic wellness and spa websites that replace expertise with soft colors and vague reassurance.
- Clinical or institutional healthcare interfaces that feel cold, diagnostic, or medicalized.
- Template landing pages with oversized headlines, repeated card grids, decorative numbering, ornamental lines, and excessive hover effects.
- Maternal branding that becomes sentimental, childish, or stereotypically feminine.
- Editorial layouts that prioritize visual drama over readable, practical information.

## Design Principles

- Show the human support behind the practice whenever the copy makes a personal promise.
- Make the visitor's current need a valid starting point rather than forcing a linear journey.
- Use calm clarity to build trust: specific services, direct actions, and honest non-clinical language.
- Preserve meaningful differences between design concepts instead of reskinning one shared template.
- Keep every interaction usable in English and Vietnamese, on mobile, with keyboard navigation and reduced motion.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA. Preserve visible keyboard focus, semantic landmarks, focus management, reduced-motion behavior, 44px mobile touch targets, readable contrast, 200% zoom resilience, and layouts without horizontal overflow at 375px, 430px, 768px, and 1440px. Both English and Vietnamese content must remain complete and usable.
