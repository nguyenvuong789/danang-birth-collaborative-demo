About the doulas

Connected care begins with listening.

The Danang Birth Collaborative brings pregnancy, birth, postpartum, movement, and nutrition support together for women and families in Da Nang.

Why a collaborative

Support should not start over when your needs change.

Pregnancy, birth, and postpartum can bring different questions at different times. A connected approach makes it easier to find the right kind of support without navigating each stage alone.

Begin with prenatal education, doula care, strength training, nutrition guidance, or a simple conversation about where you are now.

Meet your support team

Different areas of care, working as one collaborative.

Each inquiry begins with listening. From there, we connect you with the support that best fits your stage, priorities, and family.
